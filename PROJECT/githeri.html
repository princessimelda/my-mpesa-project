Succotash

Locally known as Githeri, succotash is a simple, beloved and heart warming meal that is made from corn and beans. It is a staple in many African countries especially Kenya, with each making a rendition of their own. This is a meal that can be paired with a plethora of other foods such as a chapati, some rice or an avacado. Githeri can be enjoyed anywhere and at any time.

prep time

90 min.

cook time

65 min.

serving

7 servings

Tags : Potatoes Beans Corn Food

INSTRUCTIONS

step 1

Sort, clean and soak your beans for 20 minutes then set them in a pot to boil for an hour

step 2

Remove your corn from the cob and set them in a pot to boil for 30 minutes

step 3

Once both the beans are almmost cooked through, chop up some tomatoes, onions and bell peppers. Select some spices of your choice and don't forget the salt

step 4

Add the chopped vegetables to the pot and let it simmer for 10 minutes. Add salt and your spices after the tomatoes cook down

step 5

Add both your beans and corn in and cook them for 5 minutes then add water and let it continue cooking for 15 minutes. You can add your coconut milk here

step 6

Taste and adjust the seasoning as preferred. Plate and serve.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups beans

1 cup corn

1 cup coconut milk ( optional )

2 tomatoes or tomato paste

1 onion

1 bell pepper

Salt and other spices of your choice

tools

Stainless steel pot

2 Large Heavy Pots

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups

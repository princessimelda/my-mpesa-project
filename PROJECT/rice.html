Veggetable Rice

Veggie rice is a flavorful and nutritious dish that combines rice with a variety of fresh vegetables, herbs, and seasonings. It's a versatile meal that can be enjoyed on its own or as a side dish, pairing well with curries, grilled proteins, or stir-fries. Whether made with fragrant basmati, jasmine, or brown rice, veggie rice can be customized with ingredients like carrots, peas, bell peppers, and spices to suit different tastes. Light, colorful, and packed with nutrients, itâ€™s a simple yet delicious way to add more veggies to your meal!

Prep Time

30 min.

Cook Time

15 min.

Serving

2 servings

Tags : Rice Corn Vegetables Food

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1

To a hot skillet add your cooked rice. Let it heat up for about 6 minutes.

Step 2

Add your vegetables to the rice and let them cook together for 10 minutes of medium-low heat. Add a little water if the rice gets too dry

Step 3

Drizzle some soysauce to the mixture then add some salt and any other spices you prefer. Mix it in well and ensure the soysauce is well distributed.

Step 4

Serve the rice and vegetables hot and enjoy your meal

Bonus!

If you have left over chicken or beed you can add it into the rice mixture and enjoy a full bodied meal!

ingredients

Leftover rice

SoySauce

Assorted vegetables ( steamed )

Salt and other spices as preferred

tools

Wok or Pan

Measuring Spoons

Tomato Soup

Tomato soup is a comforting and classic dish made from ripe, juicy tomatoes blended into a smooth, flavorful broth. It’s rich, creamy, and packed with a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess. Whether enjoyed as a light appetizer or a hearty meal with grilled cheese, this soup is a timeless favorite. Made with simple ingredients like tomatoes, garlic, onions, and herbs, it’s easy to prepare and can be customized with cream, spices, or croutons. Warm, nourishing, and delicious, a bowl of tomato soup is the ultimate comfort food!

Prep time

10 min.

Cook time

30 min.

Serving

4 servings

Tags : Tomato Soup Food

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1

To a hot pan add your chopped onions and sweat them out. Once done add your tomatoes

Step 2

Cook your tomatoes until they form a thick paste. Once ready, pour into a blender and blend til smooth

Step 3

Add the blended mixture back to your pot and continue to cook it. Add salt to taste, and some other spices to enhance the flavour like garlic powder and oregano

Step 4

Add your plain yoghurt or milk to the soup and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Serve while hot

ingredients

3 tomatoes

1/2 cup plain yoghurt or milk

1 onion

Salt

tools

Hand Blender

Small Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups

Chicken Wings

You know what they say about black people and chicken! Wings are a beloved meal all across the board forming a huge percentage of menus in several restaurants especially fast food spots. This is a recipe made with one of our partners, Adisia Spices, who make a range of delicious marinades and spices. Our marinade of choice in this particular recipe, is the spicy cajun marinade and spice. Try it cause you'll love it.

Prep time

Overnight

Cook time

45 min.

Serving

3 servings

Tags : Chicken Food

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1

Marinade your wings in the spicy cajun marinade overnight. If unable to amrinade overnight, 3 hours will do to ensure the flavours penetrate

Step 2

Pre-heat your oven at 200 degrees celsius for 10 minutes before placing your chicken in

Step 3

Place your chicken on a rack and let them cook for the next 45 minutes. Once ready, coat them in some cajun spice to add an extra layer of flavour

Step 4

Serve the wings while hot preferably with a side of roast potatoes and a dipping sauce of your choice

Ingredients

8 chicken wings

Spicy Cajun marinade

Spicy Cajun spice
